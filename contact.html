Everything you need to know about buying and selling cars with Cardeal.pk

Cardeal.pk is a car marketplace that connects sellers with genuine buyers. If you want to sell your car, simply list it with us and our team will advertise it across multiple platforms including Facebook and our website. We handle the promotion while you receive inquiries from serious, ready-to-buy customers. For buyers, you can browse our listings and contact sellers directly through our platform.
Yes, listing your car on Cardeal.pk is completely free! There are no upfront charges or hidden fees. We believe in a no-sale-no-fee model, which means you only benefit from our services without any financial risk. Our goal is to help you sell your car as quickly as possible through effective advertising and buyer matching.
The selling timeline varies depending on the car model, condition, price, and market demand. On average, most cars listed with us receive inquiries within the first few days and are sold within 1 to 3 weeks. Popular models like Toyota Corolla and Honda Civic tend to sell faster. Our advertising strategies are designed to maximize visibility and attract serious buyers quickly.
We use a multi-channel advertising approach to find the right buyers for your car. Your listing is promoted on our website, shared across our Facebook groups with thousands of active members, and advertised through targeted social media campaigns. We also maintain a database of buyers who are actively looking for specific car models, allowing us to match them with your listing instantly.
Absolutely. We take your privacy and data security very seriously. Your personal information is never shared publicly or with third parties without your consent. When buyers inquire about your car, we facilitate the connection in a controlled manner. We follow strict data protection practices and only use your information for the purpose of helping you buy or sell a vehicle.
While our office is based in Lahore, Punjab, Cardeal.pk serves customers across all major cities in Pakistan including Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, and Quetta. Our online platform and digital advertising reach buyers and sellers nationwide, so no matter where you are in Pakistan, we can help you buy or sell your car.
